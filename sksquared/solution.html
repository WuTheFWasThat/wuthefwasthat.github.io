First, we solve the clues, which are (slightly non-standard) cryptic crossword clues.

Clue Explanation Answer
a cadet earns ones taken from trip over seas anagram(trip + seas - a) STRIPES

dancing couples meet in cultured city anagram(pairs) PARIS

hush! one red tea fits to a tee sh + i + rt, synonym(tee) SHIRT

the beginning of winning and the end of strife? w + ife WIFE
the tide always engulfs the best substring(tide always) IDEAL

Then, motivated by the theme sksquared (the bride and grooms' shared initials), we must notice that each answer can have an existing letter replaced with a S or K. The way to do this is not unique, but there is only one way to do it that fits the answers into the crossing cell diagrams on the left, such that an S and K cross. In fact, the S word is always horizontal and the K word vertical. Unfortunately, there was an erratum where PARIS and STATE were interchangeable.

Horizontal word Vertical word
EIGHT → SIGHT PARIS → PARKS
MOON → SOON MISSED → KISSED
INJURED → INSURED SHIRT → SKIRT
REVOLUTION → RESOLUTION UNITS → KNITS
WIFE → WISE STATE → STAKE
IDEAL → IDEAS STRIPES → STRIKES


Finally, there are two extraction mechanisms
  • First, by reading from the highlighted cells in the diagrams on the left.
  • Second, by taking the displaced letters and offsetting with the numbers on the right.
This gives two phrases:
  • The horizontal words give INDRED and the vertical words give PIRITS, cluing KINDRED SPIRITS
  • The offsets applied to the displaced letters yields SECRET KEYS
